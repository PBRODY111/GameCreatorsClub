# GameCreatorsClub

Each room 15 mins to exit 
Specific times unlocked items

Intro
- New student
- Teacher (Davis?) ask to sit down
- Meet new girl
- Teacher tells them to stop talking
- Explosion, smoke + dust
- Black out
Room 1
- Locked in classroom
- Find keys
- Move specific items to reveal other items
- Padlock code
- Master key to open room
Room 2
- Locked in classroom
- Monster "arm" enters through one of five windows every 30 secs
Room 3
- Locked in lab
- Small gremlin will chase you around for 30 sec periods before resting in closets
- Find Myra in one of the closets
Room 4
- Locked in library
- Flying bat crawls across ceiling and jumps down at random
- Find series of books according to letters to open
- Myra helps fend off bat
Room 5
- Full Cerberus 
- Chase through cafeteria
- Use coins to buy items from vending machine to deflect Cerberus
Ending
- Key hanging from Cerberus’s neck
- Able to get after defending
- Unlock door
